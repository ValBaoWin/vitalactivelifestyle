Chiropractic adjustments restore proper spinal alignment, reduce inflammation, and accelerate natural healing — often avoiding the need for surgery or long-term medication. Low Back Injury / Lower Back Pain

Lower Back Pain Treatment in San Jose

Lower back pain is one of the most common reasons people seek chiropractic care — it can range from a dull, persistent ache that slows your workday to sharp, shooting pain that makes sitting or standing unbearable. Common causes include disc herniation, muscle strain from lifting, poor posture, and spinal misalignment from an old injury or accident.

Dr. Nguyen uses targeted spinal adjustments, soft-tissue therapy, and corrective exercises to reduce pain, restore mobility, and help you stay active for the long term. Many patients feel significant relief within just a few visits.

Neck Pain

Neck Pain & Cervical Spine Treatment

Whether it stems from hours at a computer, sleeping at an odd angle, or a whiplash injury from a car accident, neck pain can restrict your range of motion and radiate into your shoulders, arms, and head. Stiffness, tension headaches, and tingling in the hands are all common signs that the cervical spine needs attention.

Chiropractic adjustments to the cervical spine relieve nerve compression, ease muscle tension, and restore the natural curve of your neck. Dr. Nguyen also addresses contributing factors like forward head posture — a frequent hidden culprit in recurring neck pain — to keep the problem from coming back.

Lower Cross Syndrome

Lower Cross Syndrome Relief

Lower Cross Syndrome (LCS) is a muscle imbalance pattern common in people who sit for extended periods — think desk workers, commuters, and drivers. Overactive, tight hip flexors and lower-back extensors combine with weak, inhibited glutes and core muscles to pull the pelvis forward, creating an exaggerated lumbar curve. The result is persistent lower back pain, hip tightness, and poor posture that worsens over time.

Dr. Nguyen treats Lower Cross Syndrome by addressing the full pattern: chiropractic adjustments for the spine and pelvis, targeted release of the tight muscle groups, and activation exercises for the weak ones. This approach corrects the underlying imbalance rather than simply treating the pain.

Shoulder Injuries

Shoulder Injury & Rotator Cuff Treatment

Shoulder pain from rotator cuff strains, impingement syndrome, frozen shoulder (adhesive capsulitis), or accident-related trauma can make it painful — or impossible — to raise your arm, reach overhead, or sleep comfortably. What many patients don't realize is that shoulder injuries are often linked to dysfunction in the cervical spine and upper back, which is why treating the shoulder alone frequently falls short.

Dr. Nguyen evaluates the full kinetic chain — from the neck and thoracic spine through the shoulder joint and surrounding musculature — to identify all contributing causes and deliver comprehensive care that restores full function.

Knee Injury

Knee Injury & Knee Pain Treatment

Knee pain — whether from an accident, sports injury, patellofemoral syndrome, or gradual wear — can sideline you from activities you love. An important principle: the knee is often the victim rather than the cause. Misalignment in the hip, pelvis, or foot places uneven stress on the knee joint, and those biomechanical problems must be corrected for lasting relief.

Dr. Nguyen's approach combines soft-tissue therapy, joint mobilization, and correction of the mechanical imbalances above and below the knee. With the right conservative care plan, many patients avoid surgery and return to full activity.

Hip Injury

Hip Injury & Hip Pain Treatment

Hip pain can stem from bursitis, labral tears, sacroiliac joint dysfunction, or direct injury, and it often presents as deep groin aching, outer hip tenderness, or pain that radiates into the buttock and thigh. Because the hip connects the spine to the lower extremity, dysfunction here can trigger a cascade of problems in the back, knee, and ankle.

Chiropractic care for hip injuries addresses joint mechanics, muscle imbalances across the pelvis, and SI joint alignment to restore pain-free movement. Dr. Nguyen treats the hip in the context of the whole body — identifying and correcting the patterns that allowed the injury to develop, so you heal fully and stay that way.

Dr. Nguyen's philosophy is simple: pain is a signal, not a sentence. Whether you're a competitive athlete, a desk worker, or recovering from a car accident, his goal is to relieve your pain, help you understand why it happened, and give you the tools to keep it from returning.
