Evergreen is a community of families, professionals, and active residents who deserve convenient access to quality chiropractic care. Dr. Lam Nguyen, D.C. has been serving this area since opening VAL Chiropractic in 2010, and many of our patients come from Evergreen and the nearby neighborhoods.

Services Available to Evergreen Patients

From our San Jose location, we provide a full range of chiropractic services to Evergreen-area patients, including personal injury and car accident care, whiplash treatment, sports injury chiropractic, lower back pain treatment, neck pain treatment, sciatica care, posture correction, and soft tissue therapy. Whether you're dealing with an acute injury, chronic pain, or seeking to improve your overall function, Dr. Nguyen can help.

Why Choose VAL Chiropractic

Dr. Lam Nguyen, D.C. is a San Jose community member who has built his practice on a reputation for thorough, honest, evidence-based care. With over 20 years of clinical experience, Dr. Nguyen specializes in soft tissue injuries, personal injury cases, and sports rehabilitation. He holds certifications in Active Release Technique and the Graston Technique, and serves as a Qualified Medical Examiner (QME) for personal injury evaluations.

For patients involved in car accidents, VAL Chiropractic offers lien-based treatment — meaning you may receive care with no upfront costs, paid from your settlement. We also provide the detailed medical documentation that personal injury attorneys need to support their clients' claims.
