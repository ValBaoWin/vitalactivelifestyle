Car Accident Injuries and Chiropractic Care

Car accidents — even low-speed collisions — can cause significant soft tissue injuries that may not become apparent until hours or days after the crash. At VAL Chiropractic in San Jose, Dr. Lam Nguyen, D.C. specializes in evaluating and treating auto accident injuries, including whiplash, neck pain, back pain, and soft tissue damage.

Getting evaluated promptly after a crash serves two important purposes: it supports your recovery and creates the documentation your personal injury attorney may need.

Don't wait to be seen: Adrenaline after a crash can mask pain. Whiplash and soft tissue injuries often surface 24–72 hours later. Early evaluation protects your health and your claim.

Common Car Accident Injuries We Treat

  • Whiplash — rapid cervical flexion-extension causing muscle, ligament, and disc strain
  • Neck pain and stiffness — often the first symptom after a rear-end or side-impact collision
  • Lower and upper back pain — compression forces from impact affecting the lumbar and thoracic spine
  • Headaches — cervicogenic headaches from neck tension and spinal dysfunction
  • Shoulder pain — from airbag impact, seatbelt forces, or bracing at the moment of collision
  • Soft tissue injuries — muscle strains, ligament sprains, and fascia restrictions

How We Help After a Car Accident

Dr. Nguyen performs a thorough evaluation that includes your medical history, a physical examination, and a review of your injuries. He then develops a personalized care plan that may include spinal adjustments, soft tissue work, and rehabilitation guidance. Individual results vary — Dr. Nguyen will be straightforward with you about what chiropractic care can and cannot address for your specific injuries.

Working with Your Attorney

We have experience coordinating with personal injury attorneys and can provide detailed, deposition-ready injury records. We also work with MedPay and PIP insurance coverage. If you need an attorney referral, we can provide one upon request.

Steps After a Car Accident

  1. Get evaluated — see a healthcare provider promptly, even if you feel okay.
  2. Document the accident — photos, police report, witness information.
  3. Report to your insurer — notify your insurance company as soon as possible.
  4. Call VAL Chiropractic — (408) 532-1139 — for an injury evaluation and to start documentation.

Common Questions

Car Accident FAQs

Can chiropractic help with car accident injuries?

Chiropractic care is one of the most common conservative treatments for auto accident injuries. Spinal adjustments and soft tissue work may help restore mobility and reduce pain. Individual results vary.

What is whiplash?

Whiplash is a neck injury caused by rapid back-and-forth motion during impact. Symptoms include neck pain, stiffness, headaches, and shoulder pain — often appearing hours or days after the accident.

Do you offer lien-based treatment?

Yes, for qualifying personal injury patients, care may be billed on lien — meaning it may be paid from your settlement with no upfront costs.

How long does recovery take after a car accident?

Recovery time varies depending on the severity of the injury. Dr. Nguyen will assess your specific injuries and discuss a realistic care plan during your initial evaluation.
