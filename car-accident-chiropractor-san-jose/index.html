Car Accident Injuries and Delayed Symptoms

One of the most challenging aspects of auto accident injuries is that symptoms frequently don't appear immediately. The adrenaline and stress hormones released during an accident can mask pain for hours — or even days — after the collision. This means that feeling "okay" right after a crash does not rule out injury. Soft tissue damage, cervical spine misalignment, and joint injuries may only become apparent once inflammation sets in.

This is why we strongly encourage anyone involved in a car accident in San Jose to be evaluated as soon as possible — ideally within 72 hours. Early documentation also protects your legal claim: gaps in treatment following an accident can be used to dispute the severity of your injuries.

Common Car Accident Injuries We Treat

Dr. Nguyen has extensive experience treating auto accident injuries, including:

  • Whiplash: The most common car accident injury, involving rapid flexion-extension forces on the cervical spine that can damage muscles, ligaments, and discs.
  • Neck pain and stiffness: Often a direct result of whiplash or cervical joint compression during impact.
  • Upper and lower back pain: Lumbar and thoracic spine compression are common in both rear-end and frontal collisions.
  • Headaches: Post-accident headaches are frequently cervicogenic in nature — originating from cervical spine dysfunction and muscle tension.
  • Soft tissue damage: Sprains, strains, and fascia injuries may cause diffuse pain, swelling, and limited range of motion.
  • Shoulder and arm symptoms: Impact forces and airbag deployment can injure shoulder joints and the surrounding musculature.

Chiropractic Care for Auto Accident Recovery

Chiropractic care focuses on the musculoskeletal system — the bones, joints, muscles, and soft tissues most commonly affected by auto accidents. Dr. Nguyen's approach to accident recovery may include:

  • Spinal evaluation and targeted adjustments to address alignment issues caused by the impact
  • Soft tissue therapy to address muscle tension, inflammation, and adhesions
  • Assessment of functional limitations and range of motion deficits
  • Guidance on home care, activity modification, and rehabilitation exercises

Individual results vary, and Dr. Nguyen will discuss a realistic recovery timeline during your initial consultation.

Lien-Based Treatment Available

VAL Chiropractic offers lien-based treatment for qualifying personal injury patients. Call us to discuss whether lien-based care is appropriate for your situation.

Steps to Take After a Car Accident

  1. Ensure your safety and call 911 if anyone is injured.
  2. Document the accident scene — photos, contact information, and insurance details.
  3. Seek medical evaluation promptly, even if you feel fine.
  4. Report the accident to your insurance company.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can chiropractic help with car accident injuries?

Chiropractic care is one of the most common conservative treatments for auto accident injuries. Spinal adjustments, soft tissue work, and guided rehabilitation may help restore mobility and reduce pain. Individual results vary.

What is whiplash and how is it treated?

Whiplash is a neck injury caused by the rapid back-and-forth motion of a collision. Symptoms include neck pain, stiffness, headaches, and shoulder pain. Chiropractic care may help by restoring cervical spine alignment and reducing soft tissue inflammation.

How long does recovery take after a car accident?

Recovery time varies depending on the severity of the injury. Dr. Nguyen will assess your specific injuries and discuss a realistic care plan during your initial evaluation.
