What Is Active Release Technique (ART)?

Active Release Technique (ART) is a patented soft tissue system that treats problems with muscles, tendons, ligaments, fascia, and nerves. It was developed to address overuse injuries, scar tissue buildup, and muscle adhesions that can restrict movement and cause pain. ART providers use hands-on protocols combining precise contact with specific patient movements to identify and treat areas of dysfunction in the soft tissues.

ART is often sought by athletes, active individuals, and people dealing with overuse injuries such as carpal tunnel syndrome, shin splints, plantar fasciitis, rotator cuff injuries, and IT band syndrome. It has also been used for post-accident soft tissue injuries and repetitive stress conditions in office workers.
