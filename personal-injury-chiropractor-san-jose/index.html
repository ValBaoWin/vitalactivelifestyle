What Is Personal Injury Chiropractic Care?

Personal injury chiropractic care is specialized treatment for musculoskeletal injuries sustained as a result of an accident — most commonly car accidents. At VAL Chiropractic in San Jose, Dr. Lam Nguyen, D.C. evaluates and treats accident-related injuries including whiplash, neck pain, back pain, soft tissue damage, and headaches. Beyond treatment, Dr. Nguyen provides the thorough medical documentation that personal injury attorneys need to support their clients' claims.

Unlike general chiropractic care, personal injury cases require careful documentation of injury onset, severity, functional limitations, and treatment progress. Dr. Nguyen is experienced in this process and works collaboratively with attorneys and insurance representatives to support your recovery and your case.

Common Injuries After a Car Accident

Accidents can cause a wide range of injuries, many of which may not be immediately apparent. Common injuries that Dr. Nguyen evaluates and treats include:

  • Whiplash: Rapid back-and-forth motion of the neck during impact can strain muscles, tendons, and ligaments and affect the cervical spine.
  • Neck pain and stiffness: Often the first symptom people notice after a collision, sometimes appearing hours or days later.
  • Upper and lower back pain: Spinal compression and soft tissue injury are common in both rear-end and side-impact collisions.
  • Headaches: Cervicogenic headaches originating from neck tension and cervical spine dysfunction are frequently reported after accidents.
  • Shoulder pain: Impact forces can affect the shoulder joint and surrounding musculature, especially with airbag deployment or bracing.
  • Soft tissue injuries: Muscle strains, ligament sprains, and fascia damage may cause pain, swelling, and restricted movement.

How Chiropractic Care May Help After an Accident

Chiropractic care is one of the most commonly used conservative treatments for auto accident injuries. Dr. Nguyen's approach may help by:

  • Restoring cervical and lumbar spinal alignment affected by the impact
  • Reducing muscle tension and soft tissue inflammation
  • Improving range of motion in the neck, shoulders, and back
  • Addressing nerve compression that may contribute to radiating pain or numbness
  • Supporting the body's natural healing process with targeted, hands-on care

Individual results vary. Dr. Nguyen will evaluate your specific injuries and discuss a realistic care plan during your initial visit.

Documentation and Medical Records for Your Attorney

One of the most important aspects of a personal injury chiropractic case is proper documentation. Dr. Nguyen provides detailed injury reports, treatment records, and progress notes that your attorney can use to establish the nature and extent of your injuries, the necessity of treatment, and the impact of the accident on your daily life. Thorough, accurate medical documentation is a critical component of a successful personal injury claim.

Working with Attorneys and Insurance

VAL Chiropractic has experience working alongside personal injury attorneys and navigating auto insurance processes including MedPay and PIP (Personal Injury Protection) coverage. We communicate directly with your legal team as needed and can provide deposition-ready records. If you have an attorney, we are happy to work with them. If you do not yet have an attorney, we can provide a referral upon request.

Lien-Based Treatment — No Upfront Costs

VAL Chiropractic offers lien-based treatment for qualifying personal injury patients. This means your chiropractic care may be paid from your settlement — with no upfront out-of-pocket costs to you. Call us to discuss your specific situation and determine whether lien-based care is appropriate for your case.

What to Do Immediately After a Car Accident

  1. Seek medical attention — even if you feel okay, get evaluated promptly.
  2. Document the scene — photograph the vehicles, surroundings, and any visible injuries.
  3. Report the accident to your insurance company.
  4. Contact a personal injury attorney if you believe another party is at fault.
  5. Call VAL Chiropractic at (408) 532-1139 — ideally within 72 hours — for an injury evaluation and to begin documentation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a chiropractor after a car accident?

Even if you feel okay after a crash, soft-tissue injuries and whiplash can take hours or days to become symptomatic. A chiropractic evaluation can help identify injuries early, support your recovery, and create documentation for your personal injury claim.

How soon should I see a chiropractor after a car accident?

As soon as possible — ideally within 72 hours of the accident. Early documentation strengthens your legal claim, and prompt care may reduce the risk of injuries becoming chronic.

Do you treat personal injury cases?

Yes. VAL Chiropractic accepts personal injury cases. We evaluate and treat accident-related injuries and provide detailed medical documentation to support your attorney's work.

Do you accept lien-based cases?

Yes, we work on a lien basis for qualifying personal injury patients, meaning your care may be covered from your settlement with no upfront costs to you.

What should I expect on my first visit?

Dr. Nguyen will perform a thorough evaluation including your history, a physical exam, and review of any available imaging. He will then discuss his findings and develop a personalized care plan.
