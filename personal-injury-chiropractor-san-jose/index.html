What Is Personal Injury Chiropractic Care?

Personal injury chiropractic care is specialized treatment for musculoskeletal injuries sustained in an accident — most commonly car accidents. At VAL Chiropractic in San Jose, Dr. Lam Nguyen, D.C. evaluates and treats accident-related injuries including whiplash, neck pain, back pain, soft tissue damage, and headaches.

Beyond treatment, Dr. Nguyen provides the thorough medical documentation that personal injury attorneys need to support their clients' claims. Careful documentation of injury onset, severity, functional limitations, and treatment progress can be critical to a successful personal injury case.

Important: Soft-tissue injuries and whiplash often don't appear until hours or days after a collision. Getting evaluated promptly — even if you feel okay — protects both your health and your legal claim.

Common Injuries After a Car Accident

Accidents can cause a wide range of injuries, many of which may not be immediately apparent. Common conditions Dr. Nguyen evaluates and treats include:

  • Whiplash — rapid back-and-forth neck motion that strains muscles, tendons, and the cervical spine
  • Neck pain and stiffness — often the first symptom, sometimes appearing hours or days after the collision
  • Upper and lower back pain — spinal compression and soft tissue injury from impact forces
  • Headaches — cervicogenic headaches from neck tension and cervical spine dysfunction
  • Shoulder pain — from airbag deployment, bracing, or seatbelt forces
  • Soft tissue injuries — muscle strains, ligament sprains, and fascia damage

How Chiropractic Care May Help

Chiropractic care is one of the most commonly used conservative treatments for auto accident injuries. Dr. Nguyen's approach may help by restoring cervical and lumbar spinal alignment, reducing muscle tension and soft tissue inflammation, improving range of motion, and addressing nerve compression. Individual results vary — Dr. Nguyen will evaluate your specific injuries and discuss a realistic care plan.

Working with Attorneys and Insurance

VAL Chiropractic has experience working alongside personal injury attorneys and navigating auto insurance processes including MedPay and PIP coverage. We communicate directly with your legal team and can provide deposition-ready records. If you do not yet have an attorney, we can provide a referral upon request.

What to Do After a Car Accident

  1. Seek medical attention — even if you feel okay, get evaluated promptly.
  2. Document the scene — photograph vehicles, surroundings, and any visible injuries.
  3. Report to your insurer — notify your insurance company promptly.
  4. Call VAL Chiropractic — ideally within 72 hours — for an injury evaluation and to begin documentation.

Common Questions

Personal Injury FAQs

Do I need to see a chiropractor after a car accident?

Even if you feel okay after a crash, soft-tissue injuries and whiplash can take hours or days to become symptomatic. A chiropractic evaluation can help identify injuries early, support your recovery, and create documentation for your personal injury claim.

How soon should I see a chiropractor after a car accident?

As soon as possible — ideally within 72 hours of the accident. Early documentation strengthens your legal claim, and prompt care may reduce the risk of injuries becoming chronic.

Do you accept lien-based cases?

Yes. VAL Chiropractic works on a lien basis for qualifying personal injury patients, meaning your care may be covered from your settlement with no upfront costs to you. Call us to discuss your situation.

What should I expect on my first visit?

Dr. Nguyen will perform a thorough evaluation including your history, a physical exam, and review of any available imaging. He will then discuss his findings and develop a personalized care plan.
